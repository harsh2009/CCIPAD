About the Founder

Rev. Fr. Ikechukwu Mathias Odigbo

Fr. Odigbo is interested in providing a practical answer to one central question: which social problems can the church help to solve? This has been his driving force in his involvement in humanitarian and charity projects.

Inspired by the rich resources of Catholic Social Teaching that call us to respond to social problems in practical ways as Jesus did, he believes that the church or the Christian Community can do a lot to end extreme poverty, exploitation of workers, abuse of human rights, untimely death, maltreatment of prisoners etc.

In 2008, he founded the Christian Community Initiative for Peace and Development. Its primary mission is to empower Christian Communities at the parish, zonal, society and organisation levels to undertake projects that can bring development and progress within the communities.
