You are welcome to our website. We welcome you to hear the story of the two types of work that we do. First, we mobilize Christian communities to draw from the resources available within and around them to solve the most basic problems and challenges they face.

We realize that it is not that simple, which is why here we share examples of what we do in our own locality hoping that other Christian communities can draw from our template and try to replicate or expand on these initiatives. Here you will find resources, guidelines, frameworks, concrete examples and technical support to enable your church and Christian groups or associations to contribute in significant ways to the development and social wellbeing of the people, the community or environs where you live and work.

By Christian communities we mean dioceses, parishes, zones, devotional groups, service organisations, membership and identity sodalities, all types of associations in so far as they have Christians as members etc. Our primary aim is to inspire these groups to undertake practical development projects in the communities where they are and to provide technical support for them to do so.

We are non-profit, non-political and non-partisan. We welcome all denominations. We work for peace and all manner of developments in areas of health, education, agriculture, skills acquisition, capacity-building, trainings, etc. We invite you to navigate our site and leave us comments for improvements and to make donations to enable us to continue the work we are doing.

Who we are (our brand)

We are a faith-based organization consisting of Christian clergy and lay people working to improve the social wellbeing of the various Christian communities where we live and work throughout Nigeria.

Our purpose (why do we exist)

We exist to stimulate social development in Christian communities like churches or associations by offering advice, information, resources, and technical support. We also execute projects that further the wellbeing of Christian communities.

Our vision (what do we want to achieve)

To eliminate of extreme poverty among the Christian communities in Nigeria through the active involvement and interventions of churches and Christian groups and associations at all levels.

Our mission (how do we want to achieve it)

To offer technical support and resources to churches and Christian communities on the development of their communities and to lead by example by undertaking some development projects.

Our story (what is our background and motivation)

We are a group of Christian clergy and laity who think the church enjoys enormous support from the community and are in a good position to give back to the community in sundry ways to achieve the objectives of development and peace.
